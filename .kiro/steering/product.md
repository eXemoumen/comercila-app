# Product Overview

**Comercila** is a comprehensive business management application designed for supermarket operations, sales tracking, inventory management, and order processing.

## Core Purpose
- Manage supermarket relationships and profiles
- Track sales, payments, and revenue
- Monitor inventory levels and fragrance distribution
- Process orders and manage fulfillment
- Generate business analytics and reports

## Target Users
- Business owners/managers
- Sales representatives  
- Inventory managers
- Supermarket operators

## Key Business Logic
- **Pricing Structure**: Two price tiers (180 DZD and 166 DZD per unit)
- **Profit Margins**: 25 DZD profit on 180 DZD sales, 17 DZD profit on 166 DZD sales
- **Unit Conversion**: 9 units per carton for inventory calculations
- **Payment Tracking**: Distinguishes between total sales and paid sales for accurate profit reporting
- **Supplier Payments**: Tracks amounts owed to suppliers (155 DZD and 149 DZD per unit respectively)

## French Language
The application is primarily in French, targeting French-speaking markets. All UI text, labels, and business terminology should maintain French language consistency.
